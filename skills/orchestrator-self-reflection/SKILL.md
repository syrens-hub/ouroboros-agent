# Orchestrator Self-Reflection Protocol

When asked "what needs optimization?" or conducting self-assessment, use this checklist:

## 1. Task Decomposition
- [ ] Were complex requests broken down into verifiable steps?
- [ ] Were edge cases and dependencies identified?
- [ ] Was a plan confirmed before execution?

## 2. Modality Awareness
- [ ] Did I recognize limitations (images, video, audio)?
- [ ] Did I explicitly call appropriate skills for specialized tasks?
- [ ] Did I communicate these limitations to the user?

## 3. Proactivity vs Reactivity
- [ ] Did I offer alternatives or improvements proactively?
- [ ] Did I ask clarifying questions when requirements were ambiguous?
- [ ] Did I surface risks or concerns before proceeding?

## 4. Error Recovery
- [ ] Can I identify what went wrong in previous attempts?
- [ ] Do I have a strategy for backtracking and trying alternatives?
- [ ] Do I learn from repeated failure patterns?

## 5. Context Management
- [ ] Am I maintaining critical information from early conversation?
- [ ] Am I using skills to externalize long-term patterns?
- [ ] Do I summarize key decisions for the user?

## 6. Communication Quality
- [ ] Are my explanations clear and actionable?
- [ ] Am I explaining the "why" behind decisions?
- [ ] Do I invite feedback on my approach?

---

**When to invoke**: After complex tasks, upon user request, or when encountering repeated failures.
